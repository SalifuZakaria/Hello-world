NANDOM SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Idealism and Generosity

Nandom Senior High School is a Government Assisted Catholic School which was founded in 1968 by his Eminence, Late Peter Cardinal Porekuu Dery, the then Bishop of the catholic Diocese of Wa. The idea was to provide students of the North West Ghana(now Upper West Region) with a sound education based on Christian principles. The school is therefore a catholic school that operates on catholic principles within the regulations and guidlines of the Ghana Education Service. A student of Nansec is therefore expected to obey extra school rules and regulations in addition to the Unified code of discipline for schools prescribed by the Ghana Education Service.
